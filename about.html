Our Mission

To foster entrepreneurial thinking and innovation among students at Ramdeo Baba University by providing comprehensive support, mentorship, and resources needed to transform ideas into successful ventures. We aim to create a thriving ecosystem where creativity meets opportunity.

Our Vision

To become the leading entrepreneurship cell in India, recognized for producing innovative startups and successful entrepreneurs who contribute to economic growth and social impact. We envision a future where every student has the opportunity to explore their entrepreneurial potential.
